UCD Club/Server Directory


Hey! My name is Samuel and I am the founder/current admin of the UCD Club/Server Directory. I am a UCD Alumni and an avid supporter of this community. I started the directory as a discord server the summer the COVID-19 pandemic began. I had been exploring around and noticed that Sac State had a similar thing, except it was smaller and only listed official clubs. That gave me the idea to create the directory for UCD students and provide a place for incoming and current students to find all the resources available to them, and to help students network during a time of isolationa dn remote learning.


Throughout the years, the directory has seen exponential growth to now over 12,000 members. I'm so happy that it has been of service to many students, as my goal has always been to provide a free service that helps the Davis community. I hope I will get the honor of continuing to serve the Davis community for many more years. If you'd like to connect, you can find me on other platforms below, or you can find me on discord: Sammy#1110 or email: spetruescu@ucdavis.edu
